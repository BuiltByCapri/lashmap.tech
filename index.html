The Story

Why a non-artist built this

I have asymmetrical eyes. Every lash artist mapped them differently. Some sets looked balanced, some didn't. When I asked "why does this work?" no one could explain it.

Not because they weren't skilled. Because they were never taught the logic behind their decisions. They mapped by feel alone.

As a former educator with a background in instructional design, I knew exactly what that meant: you can't teach what you were never taught to explain. So I built the system the industry was missing — first as teaching tools, now as AI-powered software.
