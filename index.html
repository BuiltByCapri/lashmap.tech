The Story

Why this exists

I have asymmetrical eyes. Every lash artist mapped them differently. Some sets looked balanced, some didn't. When I asked "why does this work?" no one could explain it.

Not because they weren't skilled. Because they were never taught how to articulate their decisions. They mapped by feel alone.

As a former educator, I knew exactly what that meant: you can't teach what you were never taught to explain. So I did what I'm trained to do. I reverse-engineered the logic and built the framework the industry was missing.
